Data Promise

CareVoice Data Promise

A plain-language agreement for how CareVoice handles health notes, device memory, exports, and care-team access.

What We Keep

  • Confirmed voice transcripts and AI triage fields, not raw audio by default.
  • Caregiver notes, hospital workspace settings, consent records, export history, and role profile details.
  • Device preferences such as text size, accessibility mode, demo state, and encrypted local logs.

How We Protect It

  • PII redaction checks for HKID-like strings, Hong Kong phone numbers, and email-like text.
  • Local sensitive records use browser encryption where supported; cloud logs are encrypted before Firestore storage.
  • FHIR/eHRSS-ready exports require consent confirmation and include audit metadata.

Your Choices

  • Use essential device memory only, or allow care workflow memory for a smoother experience.
  • Clear local CareVoice device memory on this browser whenever needed.
  • Sign out to stop cloud sync; ask the hospital/care team owner to remove shared workspace access.
