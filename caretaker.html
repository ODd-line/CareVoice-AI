Benefits

What caregivers get

Faster daily follow-up

Voice notes become structured logs

Instead of manual note-taking, caregiver teams get timestamped voice interaction records and category labels for easier follow-up.

Bilingual support

Built for Hong Kong communication patterns

Cantonese and English voice input helps elderly users communicate naturally, reducing misunderstanding and repeated clarification calls.

Escalation readiness

Risk cues are visible early

Medication, symptom, and emergency cues are surfaced in one workflow so caregivers can prioritize urgent cases faster.

Presentation and handoff

Evidence export for teams and clinics

CSV export and session briefs make it easier to hand cases to nurses, doctors, and family members with less information loss.
