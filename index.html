Ethics + PDPO

Assistive AI with human accountability

  • Non-diagnostic disclaimer shown in the live product and pitch materials.
  • Data minimization: only care-note text, category, latency, and user-scoped metadata.
  • Access control through Firebase Auth, Firestore rules, and verified hospital accounts.
  • Bias testing plan for Cantonese elderly phrases, noisy speech, and English caregiver summaries.
